# Dataquest-project: Insights on Signups and Subscribers

In the context of my Dataquest training, I built my very first Tableau dashboard. [Link](https://public.tableau.com/views/InsightsonDataquestSignups/SignupsInsights?:language=it-IT&:sid=&:redirect=auth&:display_count=n&:origin=viz_share_link)

## Introduction
The dataset is provided by the Dataquest platform and contains fictious information on Dataquest's business: in particular, on total signups and total subscribers. This information was collected from 2016 to 2021. The dataset contains the following fields:
- Week joined
- Country
- Channel
- Persona
- Total Signups
- Total Subscribers
- Total Minute Spent

The observations are aggregated by week. The total number of observations is 185.546.

## My approach
For the analysis, I wanted to show the general trends in total signups and total subscribers overtime. I also wanted to add detail on the type of user profiles (field "Persona") and the different source of traffic for the platform (field "Channel"). In addition to that, I also zoomed in the geographic dimension by analysis total subscribers for each greographic region.
